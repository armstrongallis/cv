What is task-based demand planning?

  • Distributed task-based demand planning model empowers everyone to bid specific tasks rather than employers hiring full-time employees based on a job description.
  • Task-based demand planning is by default distributed since any person from anywhere can bid the price they want to receive to complete the task described in the demand.
